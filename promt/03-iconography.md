## 3. Iconography
The prototype uses a small set of simple line glyphs (1.8 stroke, round caps): heart, share, flag, search, pin, camera, plus, check, back, chevron, map, grid, user, shield, close, star, globe. Use a matching line-icon set (e.g. `lucide-react-native` or `@expo/vector-icons` Feather) and keep stroke weight consistent and light. The **star** is the brand mark.
